Critics Archive is a fashion brand built on documentation, not trends. We use clothing as a medium to archive cultural moments, ideas, and tensions that would otherwise be diluted or forgotten. Each garment is designed as a record — a physical artifact carrying meaning, context, and critique.

Not everything is worth preserving. We decide what enters the archive and what remains undocumented. Trends expire. Archives endure.
